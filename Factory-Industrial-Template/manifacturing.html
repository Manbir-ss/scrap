Shredder

The Shredder is thecore part of whole shredding.Hammers of the shredder beat alternately the scrap entering the cavity and tear it into standard scrap
 

The rolling roller presses the in-feeding materials with their own weight and oil cylinder the in-feeding chute is laid with wear resistant platesand installed with a guide device to enable the materials to enter to shredder smoothly, also an anti splash device is added .The drum driver motor is from Swedish HAGGLUNDS brand, which acn be reserved

The main body has a overall base with vibration absorbers under it.The absorbers are from German Gerb, which can absorb the vibration impact of the shredder during running and reduce the force on the equipment foundation

Dedusing System

After years of test and research , Lidi has optimize the design from more than 300 sets of shredder-line de-dusting sytems and sumarized a set of effective de-dusting scvhem with cyclone/bag box. Emission concentration of dust particles at outlet 10mg/m, it is only company in China that has really mastered the dry-dedusting system for shredder.
 

The Only Company who insists on insuction air at the top of shredder all the time. The suction air with gravity settling chamber at the top of the shredder can effectively extinguish sparks and avoid pipe blocking .Explosion proof design at key position effectively protect the de-dusting box

Reasonable air volume and power, energy saving and enviromental protection .The de-dusting system designed according to the different needs of owner.
